Services

What we do

Practical HR support tailored to your stage of growth—from documentation to day-to-day guidance.

HR policies & employee handbooks

Clear, legally sound policies and handbooks that reflect how you work, protect the business, and set expectations employees can actually follow.

Onboarding programs

Structured onboarding that gets new hires productive faster, reinforces culture, and reduces early turnover through consistent first-week experiences.

Compliance & risk management

Stay ahead of regulations with practical guidance on employment law, documentation, and processes that reduce exposure before issues become costly.

Performance management

Frameworks for goals, feedback, and difficult conversations—so managers lead fairly and your top performers know how they can grow.

HR audits

A thorough review of files, classifications, and practices to find gaps, fix what matters most, and give you confidence in your HR house.

Fractional HR support

Ongoing HR partnership on a flexible basis: strategic advice, employee relations, and execution when you need depth without a full-time hire.
