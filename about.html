About GroundWork HR

HR built for the way small businesses actually work.

We started Groundwork HR because small businesses deserve the same quality of HR support as large companies — without the overhead, the jargon, or the one-size-fits-all approach.

Our Story

Hi, I'm Amanda.

I've spent my career in HR working alongside startups and small businesses — and I kept seeing the same problem. Growing teams were either winging it on HR, paying for bloated consulting packages they didn't need, or waiting until something went wrong to get help.

I founded Groundwork HR to change that. My approach is simple: give small businesses access to real, practical HR expertise — on their terms, at their pace, and within their budget.

Whether you need a single policy drafted or an ongoing HR consultant as you scale, I'm here to help you build a people foundation that actually holds up.
